# v1.4.0 Ultimate Skill Manual Trigger System Design

> Date: 2026-03-08
> Status: Approved
> Goal: Add 19 unique ultimate skills (one per hero) with manual trigger, hybrid energy charge, and dedicated UI

## Background

Currently heroes have 2 auto-cast skills each. Combat lacks high-impact player agency moments. The existing `SkillQueueSystem` already supports manual/semi_auto/auto modes with `skill:manualFire` events, but there are no "special" abilities that feel distinct from regular skills.

Two proto-ultimates exist (`ultimate_holy_burst` 25s CD, `ultimate_dark_ritual` 30s CD) but are treated as regular auto-cast skills. This design introduces a dedicated ultimate system with energy-based charging and manual activation.

## Design Decisions

1. **Hybrid energy charge** (not cooldown, not damage-only): Passive 2%/s + 3% per attack + 5% per skill use + 2% per hit taken + 10% on kill
2. **1 ultimate per hero** (19 unique): Each hero gets a thematic ultimate matching their identity
3. **Separate ultimate bar UI**: 4 circular buttons above existing SkillBar, hotkeys Q/W/E/R
4. **Medium charge speed**: ~20-25 seconds to full charge in active combat

## Architecture

### Energy System

Each hero in battle tracks an `ultimateEnergy` float (0-100). Energy charges via multiple sources:

| Source | Amount | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Passive | +2/s | Always ticking during combat |
| Attack landed | +3 | Per basic attack hit |
| Skill used | +5 | Per regular skill cast |
| Damage taken | +2 | Per hit received |
| Kill | +10 | Per enemy killed by this hero |

Energy resets to 0 at battle start. When energy >= 100, the ultimate becomes available for manual activation.

### Ultimate Skill Data

Extend `SkillData` with `isUltimate?: boolean` flag. Ultimate skills:
- Are NOT auto-cast (excluded from SkillQueueSystem)
- Have no cooldown (energy-gated instead)
- Are defined in `skills.json` alongside regular skills
- Each hero's `skills` array gains a 3rd entry (the ultimate ID)

### Energy Manager: `UltimateSystem`

New file: `src/systems/UltimateSystem.ts`

Singleton managing energy state for all heroes in current battle:
- `energyMap: Map<string, number>` — heroId → energy (0-100)
- `activate(heroes: Hero[])` — register heroes, subscribe to EventBus
- `deactivate()` — unsubscribe, clear state
- `addEnergy(heroId, amount)` — clamp to 100, emit `ultimate:ready` when crossing threshold
- `useUltimate(heroId)` — reset energy to 0, execute the skill via SkillSystem
- `getEnergy(heroId)` → number
- `isReady(heroId)` → boolean
- `update(delta)` — passive energy tick

EventBus subscriptions:
- `unit:attack` → +3 energy to attacker
- `skill:used` → +5 energy to caster (filter out ultimates)
- `unit:damaged` → +2 energy to target (if hero)
- `unit:kill` → +10 energy to killer

### UI: `UltimateBar`

New file: `src/ui/UltimateBar.ts`

4 circular buttons (one per party slot), positioned above SkillBar:
- Each button: 36×36 circle with hero icon + energy ring fill (arc from 0° to 360° based on energy%)
- Glow/pulse effect when energy reaches 100%
- Hotkeys: Q, W, E, R (mapped to party slots 0-3)
- Click or hotkey fires `ultimate:trigger` event
- Greyed out when energy < 100

Visual states:
- Charging: dim icon, partial arc fill (energy color matching hero's element)
- Ready: bright icon, full ring, subtle pulse animation
- Used: brief flash, then back to charging state

### Integration Points

1. **BattleScene**: Create UltimateSystem + UltimateBar, activate with heroes
2. **BattleSystem.updateCombat()**: Call `UltimateSystem.update(delta)` for passive tick
3. **SkillSystem**: Skip ultimates in auto-cast queue; add `executeUltimate()` path
4. **BattleHUD**: Add UltimateBar above SkillBar

## Charge Speed Analysis

In a typical fight with ~4 heroes:
- Passive: 2/s × 20s = 40 energy
- Attacks: ~1 attack/1.5s × 3 per attack = ~40 energy over 20s
- Skills: ~3 skills in 20s × 5 = 15 energy
- Damage taken: ~5 hits × 2 = 10 energy

Total ≈ ~100 in 20-25s — matches "medium charge" target.
